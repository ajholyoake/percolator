Research + Education

I am interested in problems in all areas of probabilistic combinatorics, random graphs, discrete probability and percolation, especially those with a connection to statistical physics. Before coming to IMPA in 2012 I completed my PhD under the supervision of Béla Bollobás at the University of Cambridge, where I was also an undergraduate.
